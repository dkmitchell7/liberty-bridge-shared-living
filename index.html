Our Mission

Liberty Bridge Shared Living provides safe, affordable, shared housing for homeless veterans and individuals transitioning from housing instability. Our mission is to bridge the gap between homelessness and independent living through structured, community-based housing that promotes dignity, stability, and long-term self-sufficiency.

Housing Model

  • Single-Family Homes (3–4 Bedrooms)
  • 2 Beds Per Bedroom
  • Furnished Units
  • Utilities Included
  • Per-Bed Contracting Available
